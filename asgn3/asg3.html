Hi, welcome to Sherry's mini world!

It's a 32×32 voxel world with stone outer walls, brick towers, a small staircase, a few wood blocks, and a grass floor. I've hidden 5 gold blocks in the corners — walk into each one to pick it up. You can also place and break blocks wherever you want.

Please use a browser that supports "canvas"
Controls
W A S D — move forward / left / back / right
Q E — turn the camera left / right
Hold left mouse button and drag — look around (yaw + pitch, just like a regular FPS game)
F — place a brick block in the cell right in front of you (stacks up to 4 high)
G — remove the topmost block from the cell in front of you
Gold: 0 / 5
Quest: 5 gold blocks are hidden in the corners of the world. Walk into each one to pick it up!
Wow! — Day / night cycle
Just stand still for ~45 seconds and the sky cycles through midnight, sunrise, noon, sunset and back. Everything in the world dims at night and brightens at noon (the shader multiplies the final color by an ambient value I update every frame).

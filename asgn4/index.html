Hi, welcome to Sherry's lit world!

This is the same Minecraft-inspired world I built for asgn3, but now everything is lit with a Phong shader (ambient + diffuse + specular). There's a white point light that orbits the scene, a yellow spotlight shining straight down on the red ball in the middle, and a pink torus I loaded from an .obj file sitting on the grass. You can move the lights around, change the light color, toggle each light on and off, and even peek at the raw vertex normals.

Please use a browser that supports "canvas"
Controls
W S — move the camera forward / back
A D — pan the camera left / right
Buttons toggle lighting, normal visualization, and each light individually
Light X / Y / Z sliders move the point light, R / G / B sliders change its color

Sherry's Alien Planet

Welcome to a small purple-blue corner of the galaxy. You're parked above an alien surface littered with violet rock outcrops, four glowing crystal spires, and three textured "relic" cubes left behind by some earlier expedition. A low-poly spaceship hovers in the middle while asteroids drift overhead and a ringed planet sits on the horizon — the whole scene wrapped in a Milky Way skybox and dusted with thousands of floating particles.

Controls — left-drag rotates the view, scroll zooms, right-drag pans. Damping is on so the camera glides a bit after you let go.
Wow point — a two-layer particle system built with THREE.Points and BufferGeometry: 2,500 star-dust points scattered in a large shell that slowly rotates, plus 500 magenta "firefly" particles, each with its own orbit radius, speed, and bob phase, animated by writing directly into the BufferAttribute every frame and using additive blending so they glow where they overlap.
